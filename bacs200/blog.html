NFTs (non-fungible tokens) are digital assets that represent something special, whether it be a collectible item, a piece of art, or even just a digital file. These tokens are changing how communities form, sustain and operate online. Now, through the use of blockchain technology and smart contracts, items can be stored and tracked on a secure public ledger, meaning they can never be destroyed or taken away from their owner. This opens up a whole new world for collectors and artists alike, as it allows them to create and trade digital assets in a trustless and decentralized manner.
